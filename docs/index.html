How It Works

Three seconds to text

🎹
1

Hold the key

Press and hold Control + Option (+ modifiers for different modes). Recording starts instantly.

🎙
2

Speak naturally

Talk at your normal pace in any supported language. ElevenLabs Scribe v2 handles the transcription.

✨
3

Release — done

Your text appears at the cursor, gets translated by Gemini, or lands in Obsidian. You choose.

Features

Built for the way
you actually work

Every detail is designed to stay out of your way while being instantly available.

🔊

ElevenLabs Scribe v2

Industry-leading speech recognition with automatic language detection and EU data residency support.

🧠

Gemini AI Translation

Translate and polish with Gemini 2.5 Flash — fix errors, translate between languages, or clean up your speech.

📓

Obsidian Raw Dictation

Start a session and capture voice in chunks — all appended to one timestamped Obsidian note. End with Flash to synthesize.

🎵

Live Waveform Overlay

Dynamic Island–style floating pill with real-time audio levels, recording timer, and mode-aware colors.

📋

Transcription History

Browse and copy your last 50 transcriptions. Mode badges, timestamps, and one-click copy.

🎤

Microphone Selector

Choose any connected audio input device. Switch between built-in mic, AirPods, or external devices.

⌨️

Smart Text Insertion

Inserts via macOS Accessibility API for precision, with automatic clipboard fallback when needed.

🔑

Secure & Private

API keys stored in macOS Keychain. No telemetry, no accounts. Your keys, your data.

🚀

Onboarding & Setup

Guided first-launch wizard with permission checks, API key entry, and shortcut reference. Launch at login.

Built With

Lightweight, native, private

A pure Swift menu bar app — no Electron, no browser, no telemetry. Your audio goes to ElevenLabs for transcription (with EU residency option) and optionally to Gemini for polishing. Nothing else.

🍎 Swift & SwiftUI 🎙 ElevenLabs Scribe v2 ✦ Gemini 2.5 Flash 📓 Obsidian URI 🔑 macOS Keychain ♿️ Accessibility API

Requirements
