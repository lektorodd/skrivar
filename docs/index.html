How it works

Three seconds to text

No windows. No apps to switch to. Just hold, speak, release.

1

Hold the keys

Press ⌃ ⌥ together. Recording starts instantly. Add ⇧ or ⌘ for different modes.

2

Speak naturally

Talk at your normal pace. A floating waveform overlay confirms recording. Multi-language support included.

3

Release — done

Text appears at your cursor, gets polished by AI, or lands in Obsidian. You choose the destination.

Capture Modes

Four ways to capture your voice

Same base shortcut, different modifiers. Switch modes even mid-recording.

⚡ Quick Capture

⌃⌥

Transcribe and paste directly at your cursor. Fastest path from voice to text.

✦ Translate

⌃⌥⇧

Transcribe, then polish or translate with Gemini AI. Perfect for writing in Nynorsk.

⬡ Obsidian

⌃⌥⌘

Voice capture sent straight to your Obsidian vault as a new note. Hands-free knowledge capture.

⬡✦ Obsidian+

⌃⌥⌘⇧

Polish with Gemini first, then create an Obsidian note. AI-refined ideas, instantly saved.

Features

Built for the way you work

Every detail designed to stay out of your way while being instantly available.

🎙

ElevenLabs Scribe v2

Industry-leading speech recognition with multilingual support and automatic language detection.

✦

Gemini AI Polish

Optional post-processing with Gemini 2.5 Flash — fix errors, translate to Nynorsk, or polish any language.

📓

Obsidian Integration

Send transcriptions directly to your vault as timestamped notes via the Obsidian URI scheme.

🌊

Live Waveform Overlay

A floating pill with real-time audio levels appears while recording. Mode-aware colors and status.

🔑

Keychain Security

API keys stored in macOS Keychain. Never written to disk. Never leaves your machine.

🌍

Multi-language

Norwegian, English, German, French, Spanish, or auto-detect. Polish to Nynorsk, Bokmål, and more.

Built With

Native, lightweight, private

Pure Swift menu bar app — no Electron, no browser, no telemetry.
Audio goes to ElevenLabs for transcription and optionally Gemini for polishing. Nothing else.
