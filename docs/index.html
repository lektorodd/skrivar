How It Works

Three seconds to text

1

Hold the key

Press and hold Right Option (+ modifiers for different modes). Recording starts instantly.

🎙
2

Speak naturally

Talk at your normal pace. ElevenLabs Scribe v2 handles the transcription with high accuracy.

✨
3

Your text appears at the cursor, gets polished by Gemini, or lands in Obsidian. You choose.

Features

Built for the way
you actually work

Every detail is designed to stay out of your way while being instantly available.

🔊

ElevenLabs Scribe v2

Industry-leading speech recognition with automatic language detection and EU data residency support.

🧠

Gemini AI Polish

Optional post-processing with Gemini 2.5 Flash — fix errors, translate, or convert to proper Nynorsk.

📓

Obsidian Integration

Send transcriptions directly to your Obsidian vault as timestamped notes via the URI scheme.

🌍

Multi-language

Norwegian, English, German, French, Spanish, or auto-detect. Polish to Nynorsk, Bokmål, and more.

⌨️

Smart Text Insertion

Inserts via macOS Accessibility API for precision, with automatic clipboard fallback when needed.

📊

Usage Statistics

Track session and all-time transcriptions, characters typed, and Gemini token usage.

Built With

Lightweight, native, private

A pure Swift menu bar app — no Electron, no browser, no telemetry. Your audio goes to ElevenLabs for transcription (with EU residency option) and optionally to Gemini for polishing. Nothing else.
